**RICARDO MELO**  
DESIGN+RESEARCH

I’m a designer and researcher.
I hold a degree in Communication Design from the [Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Porto](http://www.fba.up.pt) and a Masters in Multimedia from the [Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto](https://sigarra.up.pt/feup/pt/cur_geral.cur_view?pv_curso_id=732).
With a background in graphic, interface and interaction design, I’m currently a [PhD student in Design](https://phddesign2015.wordpress.com) from the University of Porto and the [Research Institute for Design, Media and Culture](http://www.idmais.org), where I was awarded an FCT scholarship in order to develop my thesis on the value of serendipity in digital interactions.

For any information feel free to email me at [hello@ricardomelo.net](mailto:hello@ricardomelo.net) or via [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/ricardomelo) or [Twitter](https://twitter.com/ricardomelo).
